COVID-19. How India fares Vs other countries?

April 5, 2019 1-minute read

This is a half baked attempt at analyzing the COVID-19 situation. Annotations and making sense of the graphs to follow soon.

Rate of new infections

  • Germany and Italy appear to be on a similar trajectory with respect to spread. (Deaths and recoveries might look quite different)
  • China’s control of COVID-19 spread is surreal.
  • The spread in India appears to way slower than other countries. It could be attributed to either or both of the low testing rate and its nation-wide lockdown of three weeks.

Rate to 2X

  • Germany and Italy again on a similar trajectory.
  • Last date, from where China’s cases doubled is close to 10th of February.
  • US is not slowing down at the same rate as European countries. Not surprising as it is a big country and initial spread was only limited to a few states.

Recoveries

  • Germany’s recovery rate was slower early on, but slightly better than that of Italy now.
